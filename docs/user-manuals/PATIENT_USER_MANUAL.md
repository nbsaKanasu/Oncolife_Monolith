## Welcome to OncoLife

OncoLife is your personal health companion designed to help you track symptoms, communicate with your care team, and stay informed during your cancer treatment journey.

**Important:** OncoLife is a support tool. It does NOT replace your care team or emergency services. If you experience severe symptoms, always contact your doctor or call 911.

## 5. My Diary

Your diary is your personal health journal.

### What's in My Diary?

1. **Auto-Generated Entries** - Created after each check-in
2. **Your Personal Notes** - Entries you write yourself

### Viewing Your Diary

1. Tap the **ğŸ““ Diary** icon
2. See entries organized by date
3. Tap any entry to view details

### Creating a Diary Entry

1. Tap **"+ Add Entry"**
2. Enter a title (optional, e.g., "Feeling better today")
3. Write your notes:
   ```
   Example:
   "Nausea was less today. Was able to eat breakfast 
   and lunch. Energy still low in afternoon. Took a 
   2-hour nap."
   ```
4. **Share with Doctor:** Toggle ON if you want your doctor to see this
5. Tap **"Save"**

### What Your Doctor Sees

### Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How often should I do a check-in?**
A: We recommend daily check-ins, ideally at the same time each day.

**Q: What if I miss a day?**
A: That's okay! Just complete your check-in when you can.

**Q: Can my family see my information?**
A: No. Only you and your care team can see your data.

**Q: Is my information private?**
A: Yes. OncoLife is HIPAA-compliant and your data is encrypted.

**Q: What if I feel fine?**
A: You can still check in! Tap "I feel okay today" for a quick check.

**Q: Who sees my diary entries?**
A: Only entries you mark "Share with Doctor" are visible to your care team.
